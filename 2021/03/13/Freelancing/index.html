2.Typical Freelance careers

Programmer

​ -Many IT companys prefer outsourcing on non-core business, with the assistance of distance working, it’s a good choice to be a freelance programmer. Some programmers’ personal projects finally turned into big business.

Photographer

​ -A lot of professional photographers choose to work for themselves, since photographers often work at different places, freelanciing is common.

Youtuber

​ -Being a Youtuber have been a hot topic over the years, because it seems to be the most fun way to earn money, sometimes a fortune. Due to it’s simplicity and the growth of the internet community, an increasing number of people are jumping into this industry.
